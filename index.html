Details

The 5m wide R5108 wide format roll printer is an industrial equipment with many advantages.
- Ability to print on solid roll materials up to 5 meters wide, with the R5108 printer you can take your rightful place in the extra wide format printing market.
- Due to a specialized system for unwinding and receiving printed material with automatic tension adjustments, it is possible to print on materials such as Stretch ceilings, stretch films and much more.
- The printer is built on a heavy-duty, open-type, heavy-duty frame, which allows the equipment to last a long service life and makes it easy to maintain the printer.
- Up to 8 Ricoh Gen5 printheads are installed on the printer, the arrangement of heads and colors is in a checkerboard pattern –°MYK x 2.
- High quality of the press turns out at the expense of high frequency of work of Ricoh Gen5 heads.
- High print speed with 4 heads set in CMYK x 2 mode, the printer outputs up to 240 sqm/hour.
